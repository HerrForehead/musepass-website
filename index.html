Walk past someone and instantly discover their current music taste. Express yourself, share your tunes, and connect via Bluetooth.

Good music taste? Passed!

Seamlessly share your music taste and discover music others are listening to using low-energy Bluetooth, completely localized and private.

Passive Discovery

Keep the app in the background. As you pass by other users, you automatically log their currently playing or last played track.

Create Your Avatar

Create your own unique avatar, built upon the Avataaars library. Choose your style, colors, and clothing to represent yourself to the world around you.

Social Links

Attach a social media link right to your MusePass profile. Met someone with great taste? You're set to connect!
